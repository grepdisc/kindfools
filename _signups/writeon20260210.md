Register for WRITE ON - Home on Tuesday February 10th at 5:00pm to 8:00pm
at Unitarian Universalist Church of Buffalo (UUCB) at 695 Elmwood Ave,
Buffalo, NY 14222. Everyone is welcome.

Our lead facilitator, Hy Carrel, will be joined by Andrea With of [L'Arche Buffalo](https://www.larchebuffalo.org/).

About L'Arche Buffalo - "Build a Kinder World - Be part of a community where everyone is welcome—with or without intellectual disabilities."

About Community Canvases' Kind Fools - "Express your truth. Hear with Us."

This is an accessible space. Dinner will be provided. This workshop on the topic of "home" and the "feeling and comfort of home" will include sharing in a circle and writing and/or conversation in pairs.
